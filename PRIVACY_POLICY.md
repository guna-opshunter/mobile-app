# Privacy Policy for NexaPlay — Games & Smart Tools for Students

**Last updated: May 9, 2026**

## Overview

NexaPlay — Games & Smart Tools for Students ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles information.

## Information We Collect

**We do NOT collect any personal information.** Our app operates entirely offline and does not:

- Collect personal data
- Track your location
- Access your contacts, camera, or microphone
- Send any data to external servers
- Use analytics or tracking services
- Display advertisements

## Data Storage

All data created within the app (such as saved records for BMI calculations, age calculations, and game scores) is stored **locally on your device** using AsyncStorage. This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is not accessible to us or any third party
- Can be deleted by clearing the app data or uninstalling the app

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app contains educational games and calculators suitable for all ages.

## Third-Party Services

Our app does not use any third-party services that collect information.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
